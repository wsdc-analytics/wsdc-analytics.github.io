New Dancers (New)

The number of dancers who received their first points at an event. This metric reflects the inflow of new dancers.

Unlike the points ranking, where positions are more stable, the top by new dancers constantly changes. Events with actively growing local communities regularly appear in the top, while traditional leaders may temporarily yield positions due to various external conditions and circumstances.

European Top (Points)

BudaFest continues to dominate, but the competition below is fierce. The gap between 5th and 10th place is just 54 points. As more dancers participate across divisions, events award more points overall. When events grow at similar rates, the relative gaps between positions narrow.
